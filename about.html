About Todo App

What is Todo App?

Todo App is a simple, elegant, and powerful task management tool designed to help you stay organized and productive. Whether you're managing daily tasks, planning projects, or tracking your goals, Todo App makes it easy to keep everything in one place.

  2. Complete Tasks: Click the checkbox next to any todo to mark it as completed
